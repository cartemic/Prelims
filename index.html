Motivation


Adapted from Radulescu, M. I., Sharpe, G. J., Law, C. K., & Lee, J. H. S. (2007). The hydrodynamic structure of unstable cellular detonations. Journal of Mechanics (Vol. 580). https://doi.org/10.1017/S0022112007005046
  • OVERALL GOAL: enable more efficient combustion through the use of detonation engines
  • Will be measuring detonation wave properties using schlieren photography
  • Determining the effect of combustion species on wave structure

Motivation

  • This is roughly what the tube will look like
  • Designing a detonation tube like this takes a considerable amount of time
  • Lots of parameters to consider, which are dependent on how the gas mixture in the tube detonates

Motivation

  • First, a subsonic flame (deflagration) must be convinced to become a detonation
  • Transition to detonation is enhanced by using periodic blockages
  • Blockage ratio and run-up distance (discuss later) are important parameters

Motivation

  • For schlieren imaging, a viewing section is needed
  • Windows must be able to withstand the pressure fluctuations in a detonation wave
  • Hardware failure, if it occurs, must be predictable and safe, minimizing damage to the tube

Motivation

  • Must account for tube's response to transient presure wave
  • (based on material properties and physical dimensions)
  • Reflected shocks further increase the pressure by a factor of ~2.5
